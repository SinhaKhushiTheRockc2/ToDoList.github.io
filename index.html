    Information

  • On clicking the New Task button , You will be able to add the task.
  • After clicking the new task button, a textbox will appear.
  • Once you add the task , simply press 'Enter' and the task will be added to your task list.
  • If you want to mark your task as completed simply click on the button present on the left side of your task, if the text turns black it means your task has been completed.
  • You can delete the task by simply clicking on the trash can.
  • For further options click on the bar icon given on the left hand side of the ToDo List
  • " The new task button will only appear on the home screen. Simply click on the bar icon present on the left hand side of the ToDo list to access home."
